FAQ

Publisher FAQ

Frequently asked questions about joining and working as a publisher.

01.  How do I become a publisher?

Submit the sign-up form, share your traffic details, and our team will review your application.

02.  How long does approval take?

Most publishers are reviewed within 24–72 hours, depending on volume and complexity.

03.  What traffic sources are accepted?

We accept SEO, content, social, email, native, display, app, and community traffic that meets quality standards.

04.  How often are payments made?

Depending on your agreement, payments can be weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

05.  What is the minimum payout threshold?

Minimum thresholds vary by payment method, but we keep them publisher-friendly.

06.  Do you provide tracking tools?

Yes, you’ll get access to a powerful tracking and reporting dashboard with real-time stats.

07.  Can I promote multiple offers?

Absolutely. You can test and scale multiple campaigns across different verticals.

08.  Is there a dedicated account manager?

Yes, top publishers work closely with an account manager to unlock growth opportunities.
