FAQ

Publisher FAQ

Frequently asked questions about joining and working as a publisher.

01.  How do I get started?

Book a strategy call or submit the contact form. We’ll review your goals, channels, and KPIs, then recommend the best campaign structure.

02.  Which industries do you support?

We support fintech, finance, insurance, e-commerce, mobile apps, SaaS, gaming, travel, education, and healthcare.

03.  How do you measure campaign success?

We measure performance using CPA, CPL, ROAS, conversion rate, revenue, retention, and qualified lead volume.

04.  What reporting tools are available?

You get real-time dashboards, custom reports, attribution views, audience analysis, and campaign benchmarking.

05.  How often are campaign reports delivered?

Reporting is available in real time, with scheduled updates depending on your operating cadence.

06.  Can campaigns be scaled globally?

Yes. We run campaigns across multiple geographies with localized targeting and channel planning.

07.  How do you prevent fraud?

We use traffic quality checks, partner vetting, anomaly monitoring, and compliance safeguards.

08.  Is there a dedicated account manager?

Yes. You’ll work with a dedicated strategist who oversees launch, optimization, and scaling.
